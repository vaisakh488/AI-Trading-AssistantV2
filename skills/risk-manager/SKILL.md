# Risk Manager — Small Capital Intraday

## Position sizing for Rs. 2000–5000 budget
- 1 lot only per trade — never trade 2 lots until consistently profitable
- Max risk per trade: 20% of lot cost (e.g. Rs. 4000 lot → max loss Rs. 800)
- Daily loss limit: stop trading after 2 consecutive losses in a day

## Capital preservation rules
- Never use more than 50% of available capital in one trade
- Keep Rs. 2000 minimum buffer — never go all-in
- After a big win (50%+), take a break for 30 minutes before next trade

## Psychological rules (remind user)
- Win rate of 40% with 2:1 reward:risk is profitable long term
- A loss is normal — the SL is not a failure, it is the plan working
- If feeling emotional (angry, overexcited) → stop trading for the day
